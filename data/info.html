How to..

  • Type your Flickr or Picasa/Google+ url in the box
  • Click 'Go' and wait until the process completes
  • Expand each attributes and analyze out your photo habit
  • Click on each pie to see the related pictures

Supported URLs

  • Picasa/Google+ - public and unlisted albums only
  • Flickr - public photostream only

Known Issues

  • Not tested in all the browsers.
  • Flickr only retrieves your top 500 pictures.
